Rasam

Ingredients

  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp tamarind pulp
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tsp rasam powder
  • Â½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp pepper & cumin (crushed)
  • 2 garlic cloves (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves
  • For tempering: 1 tsp oil/ghee, Â½ tsp mustard seeds, curry leaves, pinch

Procedure

  1. Boil tamarind water with tomato, turmeric, rasam powder, pepper, cumin, garlic & salt.
  2. Simmer until raw smell goes.
  3. Temper with mustard, curry leaves & hing in ghee/oil.
  4. Add to rasam, garnish with coriander.
